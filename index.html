Koala Launcher

Koala Launcher is a simple, yet powerful Minecraft custom launcher with a strong focus on the user experience

What is Koala Launcher

Koala Launcher is a custom open source Minecraft launcher written from the ground up in electron/react. Its main goal is to make it easy and enjoyable to manage different Minecraft versions and servers, bringing the playing and modding experience to the next level!
